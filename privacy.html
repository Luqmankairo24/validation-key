# Privacy Policy for New Tanzanite Last updated: January 25, 2026 At New Tanzanite, accessible from the Pi Browser, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by New Tanzanite and how we use it. ## Information We Collect We only collect information necessary to provide services within the Pi Ecosystem, such as your Pi username (if authorized) and transaction data related to Tanzanite. ## How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - Provide, operate, and maintain our app. - Improve and personalize your experience. - Process your Tanzanite transactions. ## Data Security We prioritize the security of your data and use standard protocols to protect it within the Web3 environment.
